# Training Style

Use for:
- 教学
- 培训课件
- onboarding
- explainers
- 操作说明

## Traits

- clear and explainable
- stable hierarchy
- readable at a distance
- less decorative, more instructional
- supports step-by-step understanding

## Recommended colors

- background: white or very light gray
- primary: navy / slate / blue-green
- accent: moderate blue, green, orange for emphasis

## Image direction

Prompt hints:
- 培训风
- 清晰讲解
- 信息图式插图
- 现代但不花哨
- 适合课件

Avoid:
- heavy marketing poster feel
- overly cinematic scenes
- crowded composition

## Layout preference

Prefer:
- cover + learning objective
- left text right image
- top text bottom diagram
- process page
- FAQ / troubleshooting page
- summary page
