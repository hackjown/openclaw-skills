# Market News Brief

Collect high-impact market/business headlines and produce a concise Chinese brief.

## Workflow

1. Gather latest market news (24h default):
   - Macro/policy events (rate decisions, inflation, regulation)
   - Major company events (earnings, M&A, guidance)
   - Risk events (geopolitical, supply chain)
2. Prefer diverse sources; avoid duplicates.
3. Rank by market impact first, not clickbait.
4. Output in Chinese with:
   - 事件
   - 核心事实（1-2句）
   - 可能影响（A股/港股/美股/商品/汇率，可按相关性）
   - 链接

## Output Template

- 今日市场快讯（时间）
- 1) 事件
  - 事实：...
  - 影响：...
  - 链接：...

Default 5-8 items.
